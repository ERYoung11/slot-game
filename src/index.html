PARROT TRIVIA: VAMPIRE FINCHES: Also known as Darwin's Finches, these unusual small birds make their home on Wolf Island in the Galapagos. Their unusual behaviour of sucking blood from the Boobies seabirds is a source of food for them during the drought periods. It does no harm to the boobies. Read more about them at Islands of the Vampire Birds.
